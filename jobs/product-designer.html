UI/UX Designer - NY

Sawhorse Media, a New York technology company is seeking a multitalented Product Designer. Our company, located in the heart of SoHo, creates products that make the social web more fun and useful. We are excited to expand our product team with the right talented and passionate designer.

Muck Rack is our SaaS platform for media professionals and journalists to influence, track and measure the news anywhere around the world. It indexes everything journalists write about, to make it easy to find just the right journalist to pitch a story to.

We also produce the Shorty Awards, an annual award show that honors the best of social media by individuals around the world and some of the best social work by brands and media companies.

Our design and development team is small and nimble, so you will be partly responsible for helping us grow, enact more processes, and make design a larger part of this organization.

  • Are able to code your own work in HTML, with at least basic knowledge of SASS, jQuery, and git.
