Freelance UX or Product Designer

The Shorty Awards is looking for an experienced freelance UX designer or product designer to help update our current web app. The Shorty Awards is an annual award show that honors the best of social media by individuals around the world and some of the best social work by brands and media companies.

Responsibilities include:

  • Design and code interfaces for new features, or iterations on existing features
  • Ideate, sketch wireframes and implement those designs
  • Upgrading app functionality
  • Refresh some of the visual design
  • Hand-codes html, css (.sass)
  • Think through the experiences of the new site as a whole as a consumer
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work closely with designers and product managers
  • Highly honed ability to use emoticons and reaction .gifs in inter-office chat
  • Flexibility, great problem-solving skills, and sense of humor

Things to note:

  • We are looking to work with someone located in NYC, to meet in person occasionally
  • This is a rather large project, and we have more in the pipeline! We are looking for someone with freelance availability through April 2016

How to apply

Send us an email at jobs@sawhorsemedia.com explaining how you fit the bill for this project. Include links that demonstrate your best work, whether it's a project you created on GitHub, your portfolio, your Dribbble profile, a blog post you've written, etc. We’re looking forward to hearing from you!

  • Include links that demonstrate your work, such as your Github profile, personal site, or live projects you’ve built or worked on
  • Tell us why you’re interested in working at Sawhorse
